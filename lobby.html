🎯 HOW TO PLAY

🎯 The Heist

You're one of 100 thieves raiding a museum with a $1,000,000 prize pool. Each round, draw a card: Loot 💰, Trap 🚨, or Relic 💎.

💰 Loot & Escape Bonus

Loot cards award 20%-60% of the remaining prize pool (split among all players). 12% of each loot draw goes into an Escape Bonus Pool shared by those who escape.

🚨 Traps & Elimination

Each unique trap type increases the elimination rate per round (1st trap = 20%, 2nd = 30%, 3rd = 40%, etc.). Drawing a duplicate trap ends the game immediately—everyone loses!

🏃 When to Escape

Escape anytime to secure your loot from the previous round + your share of the escape bonus. You won't get loot revealed in the current round, so timing is critical!

💎 Rare Relics

3 rare relics are hidden in the museum. When players escape, relics are raffled among escapees (max 1 per player). Find them all for bonus rewards!

🎲 Strategy

Balance greed vs. risk! Stay for bigger loot but risk elimination or game over. Escape early for guaranteed rewards. Can you outsmart the odds?
