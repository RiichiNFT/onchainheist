🎯 HOW TO PLAY

🎯 The Heist

You're one of 500 thieves raiding a museum with a $5,000,000 prize pool. Each round, draw a card: Loot 💰, Security Threat 🚨, or Artifact 🖼️.

💰 Loot & Escape Bonus

Loot cards award 20%-60% of remaining prize pool (split among all players). 12% of each loot goes to the Escape Bonus Pool (claimable next round by those who escape).

🚨 Security Level & Elimination

Each unique security threat raises the Security Level and eliminates players each round: Level 1 = 15%, Level 2 = 30%, Level 3 = 45%, Level 4 = 60%, Level 5+ = 75%. Drawing a duplicate threat triggers the alarm—instant game over!

🏃 Score and Run

Click "Score and Run" anytime to escape with ALL your accumulated loot + your share of the escape bonus pool. Once you escape, you're safe—but you can't go back for more!

🖼️ Rare Artifacts

3 rare artifacts are hidden in the museum. When players escape, artifacts are raffled among escapees if escape rate is low enough (max 1 artifact per player).

🏆 Solo Winner

If you're the last thief standing, you win everything—all remaining prize pool money and all unclaimed artifacts!

🎲 Strategy Tips

High risk, high reward! Security Level increases danger each round. Escape too early? You leave money on the table. Stay too long? You might lose it all. Master the timing to maximize your haul!
